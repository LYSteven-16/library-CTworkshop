📢 Answer: A famous author gave a talk Wednesday afternoon, attracting 200 students — causing the unusual spike!

Analysis: Finals wouldn't happen only on one afternoon; rain keeps people indoors but won't add 200; new arrivals can't cause this scale. Pattern recognition means spotting both patterns AND anomalies, and inferring their causes.
